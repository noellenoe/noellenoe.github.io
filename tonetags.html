What are tone tags?

tone tags/indicators are things you can include with text to indicate what the tone of it is. This is very helpful for people who find parsing the tone of online messages very hard. You can think of them as more "serious" emoji. They can be used any time or place you may be communicating through text.

Some things to think about

You may not find tone tags/indicators useful, but many, many people who are neurodivergant or otherwise do, and making fun of them may be seen as problematic and borderline ableist due to how many neurodivergant people ask for the use of them. If you don't know a tone tag/indicator, simply ask.
